3. **What is the minimum viable shape of a web-tools extension that unblocks `/Users/sguslystyi/rpiv-pi/agents/web-search-researcher.md:4` (which declares `tools: web_search, web_fetch, read, grep, find, ls`), and where should it live relative to `extensions/rpiv-core/index.ts`?** The rpiv-core extension at `extensions/rpiv-core/index.ts:169` already demonstrates the `pi.registerTool` pattern with typebox schemas — a new `web_search` tool needs a `Type.Object({query: Type.String()})` schema and an `execute` callback, and `web_fetch` needs `{url: Type.String()}`.
